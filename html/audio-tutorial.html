Deep Fake Audios

Deep fake audios are manipulated audio recordings created using artificial intelligence (AI) techniques. These audios can mimic someone's voice and create realistic-sounding speech, often with malicious intent.

Examples of how deep fake audios can be used include:

  • Impersonating Someone’s Voice: AI is used to replicate a person's voice, potentially leading to fraud, defamation, or reputational damage.
  • Misinformation and Manipulation: Deep fake audios can spread false information, making it difficult for people to distinguish between real and fake recordings.
  • Financial Fraud: Attackers can use deep fake audios to impersonate company executives and issue fraudulent instructions to transfer money.
  • Privacy Violations: Personal data can be extracted through deep fake audios by impersonating a person to gain access to confidential information.
