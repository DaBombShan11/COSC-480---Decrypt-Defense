Video Detection

Video detection generally implies analyzing the video content by searching for manipulations or anomalies; these include deepfakes or synthetic media detections. It uses AI and machine learning techniques to identify inconsistencies in facial movements, lighting, and mismatched audio.

Key methods for identifying deepfake videos include:

  • Face Forensics: Examines facial features, expressions, and head movements for unnatural behavior.
  • Visual Artifacts: Anomalies apparent during visual representation in imagery.
  • Analysis: Identifies discrepancies like irregular blinking or edge artifacts.
  • Audio-Visual Syncing: Checks for inconsistencies between speech and lip movements.
  • Deep Learning Models: Trained on real and manipulated videos, these models can identify tampered media with high accuracy.
